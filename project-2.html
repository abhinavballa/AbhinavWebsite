Project Overview

Created a model that predicts the amount of packages FedEx drivers will drop off on a given day based on tendencies with weather, terrain, and past drop-off data. Used Sci-Kit Learn and Tensorflow to create a model and learn based on csv of data for each driver. Next step is to use model to assign certain drivers to certain routes based on characteristics of the route given and tendencies of the driver.
